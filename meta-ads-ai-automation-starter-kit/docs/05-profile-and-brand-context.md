# 05 - Profile And Brand Context

AI performs better when it understands the brand.

Use this profile format inside your prompt or workflow config.

## Example Brand Profile

```text
Brand name:
Waikato Coffee

Category:
Coffee brand

Social links:
Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/waikato.coffee/
TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/@waikatocoffee?lang=en-GB

Primary goal:
Grow awareness, drive engagement, and prepare paid campaign testing.

Audience:
Coffee lovers, local customers, cafe visitors, lifestyle buyers, and people interested in quality coffee.

Tone:
Warm, modern, simple, premium but friendly.

Creative style:
Short-form videos, lifestyle coffee moments, product closeups, behind-the-scenes, customer reactions, offers, and seasonal content.

Automation goal:
Use AI to analyze ads, protect budget, find winners, and suggest content ideas.
```

## Why This Matters

Without brand context, AI gives generic marketing advice.

With brand context, AI can connect ad performance to:

- Creative direction
- Content ideas
- Audience fit
- Brand tone
- Campaign decisions
